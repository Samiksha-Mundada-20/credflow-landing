Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026 · Effective immediately

The short version: CredFlow never reads, stores, or transmits any of your message content. We only count message sends and store two utilization numbers (0.0 – 1.0 floats) per sync. That is the complete extent of what we collect.

1. Who We Are

CredFlow ("CredFlow", "we", "us", or "our") is a Chrome browser extension and web dashboard that tracks AI chat usage limits. This policy explains how we handle data collected through the CredFlow extension, the CredFlow web dashboard at credflow-dashboard.vercel.app, and this landing site at credflow.vercel.app.

2. What We Collect

2.1 Usage data (extension)

When you use the CredFlow extension on claude.ai, we detect message send events and read two floating-point numbers from Claude's internal session state:

  • session_utilization — your current session usage as a number between 0.0 and 1.0
  • weekly_utilization — your current weekly usage as a number between 0.0 and 1.0

We also store the timestamp of each capture so we can show you usage history and reset countdowns.

We never read, store, or transmit the content of any message you send or receive.

2.2 Account data

When you create a CredFlow account, we store your email address and a hashed password (via Supabase Auth). We do not store plain-text passwords.

2.3 Waitlist data

If you join our waitlist, we store your email address and the timestamp of your signup. We use this only to notify you when the extension launches.

2.4 Technical metadata

We store a selector_version and basic extension health data (last sync time, consecutive failures) to detect when the extension breaks due to DOM changes on claude.ai. No browsing history or page content is included.

3. What We Do Not Collect

  • Message content — never, under any circumstances
  • URLs visited (beyond detecting that you are on claude.ai to trigger counting)
  • Keystrokes or clipboard content
  • Device fingerprints or advertising IDs
  • Information from any site other than claude.ai

4. How We Use Your Data

  • To show you your usage — session %, weekly %, and reset times in the popup and dashboard
  • To send alerts — Chrome notifications at 80% and 95% of your usage limit
  • To improve accuracy — historical data helps us show 7-day charts and usage trends
  • To contact waitlist signups — one email when the extension is available on the Chrome Web Store

We do not sell your data. We do not use it for advertising. We do not share it with third parties except as described below.

5. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored on Supabase (hosted on AWS ap-south-1, Mumbai). Every row in every table is protected by Row-Level Security (RLS) — your data can only be read by your own account. All communication between the extension, dashboard, and our backend uses HTTPS.

6. Data Retention

  • Free tier: Usage snapshots are kept for 90 days, then deleted automatically.
  • Pro tier: Usage snapshots are kept for 1 year.
  • Account data: Retained until you delete your account.
  • Waitlist data: Deleted after the launch notification is sent, or on request.

7. Chrome Extension Permissions

The CredFlow extension requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • storage — to cache your usage data locally for fast popup loading
  • tabs — to detect when you navigate to claude.ai
  • notifications — to send you 80% and 95% usage alerts
  • alarms — to schedule periodic JWT token refresh and background syncs

We do not request history, bookmarks, cookies, webRequest, or any permission that would allow reading content from arbitrary websites.

8. Your Rights

You can, at any time:

  • Delete your account — email us at hello@credflow.app and we will delete all data associated with your account within 7 days.
  • Export your data — request a CSV of your usage history.
  • Remove from waitlist — reply to any waitlist email or email us directly.
  • Disable notifications — via the extension popup settings at any time.

9. Children

CredFlow is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has provided us data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify users by email. Continued use of CredFlow after changes constitutes acceptance.

11. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us: hello@credflow.app

We aim to respond within 48 hours.
